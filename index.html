About Us

After having children, we left the big bad city and moved to Westchester. Like you, we were soon welcomed by ConEd bills that regularly exceeded $500.

In our research, we discovered that you could potentialy lower your bill by switching to another supplier. Unfortunately, that process was filled with complexity and hidden fees. As a result, we created Dwindle to help break through the confusion.

Dwindle is free to use and we'll never share your data with anyone.

How It Works

Our proprietary algorithm analyzes your past ConEd bills and compares them against all of the available energy suppliers in your area. Our analysis cuts through the complex pricing models, hidden fees, and taxes to reveal how much you could save. This process can take up to a couple of hours - bear with us, its worth it!

We'll continue to monitor your account each month and recommend the best plan for you from our database of energy suppliers.
